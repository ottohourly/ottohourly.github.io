Easy lending for owners

Otto Hourly Insurance is a short-term standalone product that sits alongside your annual policy.

Borrower has their own policy

This means you can lend your car to a friend or family member happy in the knowledge that the person borrowing your car has their own comprehensive insurance policy and, if they need to make a claim, it won't make your rates skyrocket .

You can have a policy with any insurer

Anyone borrowing your vehicle can get Otto Hourly Insurance regardless of which company provides your main insurance policy.
